FAQ

Welcome to frequently asked questions

What is the purpose of this website?

This website is a platform for students to find and share notes, past papers, and other resources for their courses.

How do I search for a document?

Click on the "Search" button in the navigation bar and enter the keywords for the document you are looking for. You can filter the search results by course, semester, and document type.

How do I contact the website administrator?

Click on the "Contact Us" button in the navigation bar and fill in the contact form with your message. The website administrator will get back to you as soon as possible.
